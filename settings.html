Privacy

EnergyCheck is designed with user privacy in mind.

This is a front-end prototype — no data is collected or stored on any server. In a live version, all preferences would be saved locally in your browser only, never sent to a server or shared with third parties.

EnergyCheck does not use tracking cookies. Any data entered is used only to personalise your comparison results on your own device.

About EnergyCheck

EnergyCheck helps Australian renters understand their household electricity usage and take simple steps to reduce their bills without feeling overwhelmed.
