Quick Wins — 6 Easy No-Cost Changes

These 6 actions cost nothing and can be done today. Small habits can still make a difference over time.

  • Turn off lights when leaving a room — free
  • Use fans before turning on air conditioning — free
  • Unplug phone chargers when not in use — free
  • Run full loads in the washing machine — free
  • Open curtains during the day to use natural light — free
  • Switch off the TV at the wall instead of leaving it on standby — free
