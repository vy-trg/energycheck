Alerts

EnergyCheck monitors your usage patterns and flags anything unusual. Each alert includes a suggested action to help you respond quickly without feeling overwhelmed (FR9, NFR5).

Peak Hour Usage

Suggested Action: Run washing machines and dishwashers later in the evening if possible.
