Why is usage higher in winter?

June–August are winter months in Queensland. Heating appliances such as electric blankets and reverse-cycle air conditioners are a common reason for higher electricity use during this period. This is normal for most households — your usage pattern is typical for a 2-person household in South East Queensland.
