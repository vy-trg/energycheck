About EnergyCheck

Our Purpose

EnergyCheck helps you to track daily electricity usage, compare energy consumption patterns, and discover actionable tips to lower your bills and energy usage.

Tracking Energy

Nowadays, energy bills always show a increase for seemingly no reason at all. This website provides a solution to that issue. By tracking your energy usage you can see whether your energy usage is low, medium or high compared to other households. You'll also learn about what you can control and cannot control in regards to lowering your energy usage. Tips are also given for further clarity on what you can do to help your energy expenditure.
