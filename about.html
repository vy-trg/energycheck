About EnergyCheck

A simple, renter-friendly tool for understanding your household electricity usage.

Our Purpose

EnergyCheck helps Australian households — especially renters — understand their electricity usage without feeling overwhelmed. Many people only notice a problem when a high bill arrives, and existing tools often present complex data that is hard to act on.

EnergyCheck replaces raw numbers with simple colour-coded indicators, honest comparisons with similar households, and practical tips that work within the constraints of renting.

No login required. No technical jargon. Just clear, useful information.

How It Works

1. Check your status

The Home page shows your current electricity usage with a simple green, amber, or red indicator so you know at a glance whether your usage is low, medium, or high.

2. Compare fairly

The Comparison page shows how your usage stacks up against similar households — not just the average of everyone, but households like yours.

3. Take action

The Tips and Control pages give you realistic, renter-appropriate advice — things you can actually do without needing landlord approval.

Built for Renters

Unlike many energy tools, EnergyCheck is designed specifically with renters in mind. We understand that renters cannot always change insulation, replace appliances, or install solar panels — so every tip and recommendation focuses on what you can control.

EnergyCheck does not collect or store your personal data. All preferences stay on your device only.

The Team

EnergyCheck was designed and built as a university group project at Griffith University, applying the full Design Thinking process from empathy research to working prototype.
